Bitcoin in Space!

Dunvegan Space Systems is an early stage company focused on "next mile" space transport and energy systems, spacecraft and natural resources outside low earth orbit (LEO), and other infrastructure necessary to build a sustainable transport system between planetary bodies of our solar system.

Currently, DSS's main project is Bitsat, which aims to provide bitcoin blockchain data to an unlimited number of ground receivers from a satellite in space.
